How to Get Gmail App Password:
Step-by-Step Guide:
  1. Go to Google Account
  2. Click "Security" on the left
  3. Enable "2-Step Verification" if not already enabled
  4. Under "Signing in to Google", click "App passwords"
  5. Select "Mail" as the app
  6. Select "Other (Custom name)" and enter "Phishing Detector"
  7. Click "Generate"
  8. Use the 16-character password above
Important Notes:
  • App passwords are more secure than your regular password
  • You can revoke app passwords anytime
  • Works even with 2-Step Verification enabled
  • No access to your main password
